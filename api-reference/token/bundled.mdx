## Overview

Analyzes token holder distribution to detect concentration risks. High holder concentration is a major red flag indicating potential for coordinated dumps or rug pulls.

## What is "Bundling"?

Bundling refers to a common Solana scam tactic where a developer:
1. Creates a token
2. Buys the supply across multiple wallets (bundles)
3. Makes it look like organic distribution
4. Dumps all wallets simultaneously

This endpoint detects such patterns.

## Key Metrics Analyzed

### Concentration Metrics
- **Top 10 holders** - Percentage of supply held
- **Top 20 holders** - Broader concentration view
- **Gini coefficient** - Distribution inequality measure
- **Effective holder count** - Adjusted for concentration

### Warning Thresholds

| Metric | Safe | Warning | Danger |
|--------|------|---------|--------|
| Top 10 holders | <30% | 30-50% | >50% |
| Top 20 holders | <40% | 40-60% | >60% |
| Unique holders | >1000 | 100-1000 | <100 |

## Red Flags 🚨

- **Few holders with large %** - 5 wallets holding 80%+ of supply
- **Similar wallet ages** - All created around the same time
- **Similar holding amounts** - Suspiciously even distribution
- **Known bundler addresses** - Wallets linked to previous scams

## Use Cases

- **Pre-purchase screening** - Avoid concentrated tokens
- **Market manipulation detection** - Identify pump & dump setups
- **Due diligence** - Verify organic vs artificial distribution

## Healthy Distribution Example

A legitimate token typically shows:
- ✅ Top 10 holders: 15-25%
- ✅ Gradual concentration drop-off
- ✅ 500+ unique holders
- ✅ Mix of old and new wallet ages

## Important Notes

- Newly launched tokens naturally have higher concentration
- Some legitimate tokens (DAOs, gaming) may have designed concentration
- Always cross-reference with other security checks
