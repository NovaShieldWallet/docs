title: 'Rug Pull Probability Score'
openapi: 'GET /token/rug-score/{mint}'
---

## Overview

Get a focused rug pull probability score for a Solana token. This endpoint is optimized for speed and returns a clear risk assessment without the full details of the comprehensive security analysis.

## Use Cases

- **Quick checks** - Fast risk assessment before trading
- **Trading bots** - Automated rug pull screening
- **High-volume scanning** - Check multiple tokens efficiently

## Response Fields

### Risk Score
- **score** - Rug pull probability (0-100, higher = more dangerous)
  - 0-20: SAFE
  - 21-40: LOW risk
  - 41-60: MEDIUM risk
  - 61-80: HIGH risk
  - 81-100: CRITICAL risk

### Assessment
- **level** - Risk classification (SAFE, LOW, MEDIUM, HIGH, CRITICAL)
- **verdict** - Clear, actionable verdict
- **canTrade** - Trading recommendation (boolean)

## Best Practices

For tokens with HIGH or CRITICAL scores:
- ⚠️ Do not trade without further investigation
- 🔍 Use the comprehensive `/token/security/{mint}` endpoint for details
- 📊 Check holder distribution with `/token/bundled/{mint}`
