## What Is Private Cash?

Private Cash lets you send SOL with zero trace. Transactions are processed offline for maximum privacy.

## How It Works

1. Select Private Cash from your wallet
2. Enter recipient and amount
3. Transaction is processed offline
4. SOL arrives with no on-chain connection to your wallet

## Fee

**1% fee** on all Private Cash transactions to maintain the privacy infrastructure.

## Privacy Features

**Zero trace** - No on-chain link between sender and recipient  
**Offline processing** - Enhanced privacy protection  
**Complete anonymity** - Transaction graph analysis resistant  

## Use Cases

- Private payments
- Protecting financial privacy
- Breaking transaction history trails
- Anonymous donations
