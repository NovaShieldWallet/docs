## Accessing Help Center

In the Nova Wallet app, navigate to **Settings → Support → Help Center** to access resources and support.

## What You'll Find

- Frequently asked questions
- Troubleshooting guides
- Feature documentation
- Contact support options

## Need More Help?

If you can't find what you're looking for, contact our support team at [support@novashield.space](mailto:support@novashield.space).
