## Accessing Help Center

In the Nova Wallet app, navigate to **Settings → Support → Help Center** to access resources and support.

## What You'll Find

- Frequently asked questions
- Troubleshooting guides
- Feature documentation
- Contact support options

## Need More Help?

If you can't find what you're looking for, contact our support team using the Support form:

[https://tally.so/r/zxj40g](https://tally.so/r/zxj40g)

If you cannot access the form, you can reach us at [hi@nshield.org](mailto:hi@nshield.org).
