## What Is a Watch Wallet?

A watch wallet lets you monitor any Solana address without importing its private keys. Perfect for tracking portfolios or monitoring other wallets.

## How to Add a Watch Wallet

1. Tap the **info icon** when viewing a watch-only wallet
2. Enter any Solana public address
3. View balance and transaction history

## What You Can Do

View balance and holdings  
See transaction history  
Monitor token prices  

## What You Can't Do

Cannot send transactions  
Cannot sign messages  
Cannot access private features  

Watch wallets are read-only for security.
